# Founder Research Memo Pencil Design

## Goal
Redesign the V1 homepage in Pencil using an Anthropic-like warm editorial direction while preserving Thomas Chiu's identity as a founder-builder with visible research depth.

## Strategic Positioning
- Primary identity: founder-builder
- Supporting identity: investor and trader
- Core differentiator: research depth
- Primary work anchor: agentOS

## Chosen Direction
Use a `Founder Research Memo` aesthetic:
- warm ivory background
- restrained editorial typography
- generous whitespace
- minimal UI chrome
- calm, precise trust signaling

This should feel closer to a high-end research memo or institutional founder brief than a startup landing page or a technical dashboard.

## Information Architecture
1. Hero
2. Current Work
3. Research Lens
4. Operating Principles
5. Closing Contact

## Hero Direction
- Eyebrow should explicitly state `Founder-builder, investor, trader`
- Headline should present a thesis around systems, judgment, and execution
- Supporting copy should frame agentOS as the strongest expression of the thesis
- Replace large abstract visual emphasis with a quieter memo-like `agentOS` note artifact

## Visual System
- Background: warm off-white / ivory
- Text: warm charcoal
- Lines: subtle gray-beige rules
- Accent: minimal muted slate-blue
- Display type: refined serif
- Supporting UI type: restrained sans

## Section Guidance

### Hero
- Keep one strong headline block
- Reduce technical decoration
- Present `agentOS` as a research-backed operating system note

### Current Work
- Replace generic module list feeling with 3 narrative work blocks
- Make `agentOS` visually primary
- Keep other two projects subordinate but legible

### Research Lens
- Present 3 research directions, not a dashboard or knowledge board
- Use cleaner editorial cards and fewer lines

### Operating Principles
- Reduce density
- Read like short founder principles, not a long essay wall

## Success Criteria
- The page should read as calm, intelligent, and premium
- Visitors should understand Thomas quickly as a founder-builder with research depth
- The design should feel Anthropic-like in restraint, not derivative in composition
